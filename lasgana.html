discription

This classic lasagna is a hearty, comforting Italian dish made with layers of tender pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella.The meat sauce typically features ground beef or a mix of beef and pork, slow-cooked with garlic, onions, tomatoes, and Italian herbs. Between each layer of sauce and pasta, generous amounts of cheese add creaminess and depth of flavor. Baked until bubbly and golden on top, this dish is perfect for family dinners, gatherings, or meal prepping. Serve it with a side of garlic bread or a fresh green salad for a complete, satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • lasgan noodles
  • round beef
  • chopped tomato
  • ricotta cheese
  • onion
  • garlic

steps

  1. Cook the Meat Sauce Sauté chopped onion and garlic in olive oil. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and seasonings (basil, oregano, salt, pepper). Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg, Parmesan, salt, pepper, and optional parsley or basil.
  3. Cook the Lasagna Noodles Boil noodles in salted water until al dente (or skip this step if using no-boil noodles). Drain and set aside.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna In a greased baking dish, layer: Meat sauce Noodles Ricotta cheese mixture Mozzarella Repeat layers (2–3 times), ending with meat sauce and mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25–30 minutes. Uncover and bake another 10–15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  6. Rest and Serve Let lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before cutting. Serve warm with salad or garlic bread.
