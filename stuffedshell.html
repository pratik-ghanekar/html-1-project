Stuffed Shells

Home

discription

Stuffed shells are an elegant yet easy Italian-inspired dish. Large pasta shells are filled with a creamy, savory mixture of ricotta cheese, spinach, Parmesan, and seasonings. Arranged neatly in a baking dish and topped with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella, the result is a dish that’s light, cheesy, and deeply satisfying. It’s perfect for vegetarians and ideal for entertaining because it looks impressive but is simple to make. Serve with a crisp salad or garlic bread for a wholesome meal.

Ingredients

  • Jumbo pasta shells
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Spinach
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Egg
  • Marinara sauce

steps

  1. Boil the Shells Cook jumbo shells in salted water until just tender. Drain and let cool slightly.
  2. Prepare the Filling Mix ricotta, spinach, egg, Parmesan, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Stuff the Shells Fill each shell with a spoonful of the ricotta-spinach mixture.
