Baked Ziti

Home

discription

Baked Ziti is the ultimate comfort food—warm, cheesy, and packed with flavor. It’s made with tube-shaped pasta (ziti), which holds onto rich meat sauce and creamy cheese in every bite. Unlike lasagna, there's no need to layer carefully—just toss, bake, and enjoy. The combination of tender pasta, savory meat, tangy tomato sauce, and gooey melted mozzarella makes it a guaranteed family favorite. It’s perfect for busy weeknights, potlucks, or make-ahead meals, as it reheats beautifully and tastes even better the next day.

Ingredients

  • Ziti pasta
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Garlic and onion

steps

  1. Cook Pasta Boil ziti in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the Meat Sauté onion and garlic, add ground beef or sausage, and cook until browned. Stir in marinara sauce. Simmer 10 minutes.
  3. Mix Cheeses In a bowl, combine ricotta with half of the Parmesan and some mozzarella. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Combine Pasta & Sauce Mix the pasta with some of the meat sauce and cheese mixture.
  5. Assemble & Bake In a baking dish, layer: pasta mixture, meat sauce, remaining mozzarella and Parmesan. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25–30 minutes.
